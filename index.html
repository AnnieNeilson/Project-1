What Is Wild Swimming?

Wild Swimming is just as it sounds, swimming in the wild! Our Club swims at the beaches of South Shields, though wild swimming can be in lakes or rivers, anywhere in nature!

Who Can Be A Wild Swimmer?

Anyone can be a wild swimmer! Do you love the sea? Love trying new things? Wild swimming is accessible to all, whether you're training to cross the Channel or simply want an invigorating dip.

What Do You Need For Wild Swimming?

There is a lot of gear out there for wild swimming, from wet suits to swimming bags, but the truth is a swimming costume and a little bravery is all you need to get started!
