What Is Wild Swimming?

Wild Swimming is just as it sounds, swimming in the wild! Our Club swims at the beaches of South Shields, though wild swimming can be in lakes or rivers, anywhere in nature! (re-write)


Who Can Be A Wild Swimmer?

Anyone can be a wild swimmer, do you love the sea, love adventure, love healthy splashy fun (re-write)


What Do You Need For Wild Swimming?

A swimming costume and a big jump?? Other equiptment can come in handy, for more information check out our social media sites and our blog.(re-write)
